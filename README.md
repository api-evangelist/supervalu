# SuperValu

SuperValu was one of the largest grocery retail and wholesale distribution companies in the United States before being acquired by United Natural Foods (UNFI) in 2018 for $2.9 billion. SuperValu operated retail grocery stores under multiple banners and provided wholesale distribution services. The supervalu.com domain now redirects to UNFI's wholesale food distribution platform. Suppliers interact with UNFI/SuperValu distribution through EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) and the myUNFI supplier portal.

## APIs

No public developer APIs have been documented for SuperValu. The company was acquired by UNFI in 2018, and supplier integration is handled through EDI (transactions 875, 880, 824) and the myUNFI supplier portal rather than a public REST API.
